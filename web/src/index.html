How to Get Your DOJ Cookies

  1. Visit https://www.justice.gov/epstein/doj-disclosures in your browser
  2. Click "Are you over 18" to pass the age verification
  3. Press F12 to open Developer Tools
  4. Go to the Application tab → Cookies → https://www.justice.gov
  5. Find the "ak_bmsc" cookie and copy its entire value
  6. Paste the ak_bmsc value into the form above and click "Save Cookies"
  7. The justiceGovAgeVerified cookie will be automatically set to "true"
